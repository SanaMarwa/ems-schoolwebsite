Our Montessori Philosophy

We believe each child is naturally motivated to learn. Our teachers guide children to choose purposeful activities, build concentration, and develop independence at their own pace.

  • Mixed-age classrooms that encourage peer learning.
  • Hands-on materials for practical life, language, math, and culture.
  • Respectful, calm routines that support emotional growth.

Programs for Early Childhood

Toddler Community

18 to 36 months. Gentle transitions, language-rich routines, and practical life foundations.

Primary Program

3 to 6 years. Child-led exploration in math, literacy, sensorial, and cultural studies.

Extended Care

Flexible before/after care with outdoor play, enrichment activities, and community building.

A Joyful School Community

From garden work to storytelling circles, our days are filled with rich experiences that nurture concentration, empathy, and a lifelong love of learning.
